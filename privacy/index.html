Privacy notice.

A plain-language explanation of how the franchise inquiry form works today.

Information you enter

The inquiry form asks for your name, Philippine mobile number, optional email address, planned location, preferred brand, package or budget, and whether your capital is ready.

What happens before you send

The website prepares an inquiry inside your browser. It does not automatically transmit the information to Son & Sol, store it in a website database, use cookies, or save it to local storage.

When you choose email

If you select “Open email app,” your device opens a draft addressed to Son & Sol Sunrise Marketing. Your email provider and the recipient's email provider may then process and retain the message under their own policies.

When you choose copy

If you select “Copy details,” the prepared inquiry is placed on your device clipboard. You control where it is pasted or sent.

Purpose and access

Inquiry details sent to the company are used to respond to your franchise questions, discuss package availability, and coordinate next steps. Access should be limited to authorized Son & Sol personnel handling franchise inquiries.

Your choices

You may decide not to send the prepared inquiry. If you already sent an email and want to correct or request deletion of your information, contact sonandsolsunrisemarketingmain@gmail.com.

Changes to this notice

This notice must be updated before the website enables automatic form submission, analytics, cookies, or another system that stores inquiry information.
