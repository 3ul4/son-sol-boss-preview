03 · Before you commit

Questions worth asking.

Use these answers as guidance, then confirm the terms that apply to your selected brand and package.

Is the franchise fee a one-time payment?

The current presentation describes the listed packages as one-time payments. Final terms are stated in the franchise agreement.

Are there royalty fees or quotas?

The current package presentation states no royalty fee and no quota. Confirm the applicable terms for your chosen package before signing.

Is training included?

Yes. The current package materials include product training, with the exact format depending on the selected brand and package.

How long does setup take?

The schedule depends on the package, location readiness, release of items, training, and opening coordination. The team will confirm a timeline during inquiry.

Are promotional prices always available?

No. Promo validity, package availability, and final inclusions must be confirmed directly with Son & Sol.
